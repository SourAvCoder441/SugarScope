Understanding Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body processes blood sugar (glucose). There are two main types:

Normal Blood Sugar Ranges

  • Fasting: 70-99 mg/dL (3.9-5.5 mmol/L)
  • Before meals: 70-130 mg/dL (3.9-7.2 mmol/L)
  • After meals (2 hours): Less than 140 mg/dL (7.8 mmol/L)
  • HbA1c: Less than 5.7%

Risk Factors

  • Family history of diabetes
  • Overweight or obesity
  • Physical inactivity
  • High blood pressure
  • Age over 45
  • Gestational diabetes during pregnancy
